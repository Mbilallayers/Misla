BOYS ACADEMIC LEADERSHIP ACADEMY

Students Code & Build Websites For Local Businesses

MISLA’s Coding Bootcamp is designed specifically for aspiring web designers to learn how to develop and maintain company websites. Students learn web design software and how to utilize industry hacks to build innovative websites for local businesses. By the end of the Bootcamp, students learn to collaborate with local businesses to create a published website.

Learn to Build a Website in 6 Weeks

Middle Schoolers at Boys Academic Leadership Academy in South LA will learn coding by building low-cost websites for local South LA businesses. In partnership with VSEDC (Vermont Slauson Economic Development Corporation), BALA students and MISLA volunteers will collaborate with VSEDC businesses to develop their websites.

If you are a local South LA business, Contact vsedc to learn how to become a client & apply for the program.

BALA students will learn:

  • basic understanding of HTML, CSS, & Javascript.
  • to easily edit text in html and add pictures to a website.
  • how to customize colors and fonts in CSS.
  • to program a site to accept payments for products and services.
  • to integrate with paypal’s merchant services.
  • to use Font Awesome to change icons quickly within the code.
  • to link a website to social media pages or other online pages.

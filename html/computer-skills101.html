Intro to Computer Skills :

Cover the basics of computer software, practices, and skills

Keeping up in today's job market requires more computer skills than ever including smartphones, tablets, laptops/desktop computers, and using the internet. Believe it or not, you're not the only one that still hasn't mastered computers or using them in everyday life whether that means your keyboard typing skills or just searching around online.

LEARN COMPUTER SURVIVAL SKILLS IN JUST 4 CLASSES

Many tasks are overwhelming unless you have proper training or prior experience, and even then seemingly basic tasks can still take excessive time or leave you stranded! With this course our Silicon Beach & Hollywood Studio/Lab experts not only prepare you for this workforce of the future, but also teach skills to run your own business and life in a digital world. (You will also be introduced to open source & Linux software.)

  • Students will learn
  • Typing
  • using a mouse and touch
  • Industry standard ways of sending emails
  • searching on the internet
  • using Google/Gmail/Drive
  • creating a word document/spreadsheet
  • other essential business skills for today's modern workers.

We are accepting students 18 & over. Minors under 18 must be accompanied by a supervising adult. Must be 10 or older to apply. Full and partial scholarships are available in exchange for volunteer work and apprenticeship.

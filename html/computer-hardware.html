Computer Hardware

Intro to Computer Hardware ,Learn Computer Hardware By Assembling Your Own Machine

In this class you will cover the basics of computer hardware. By the end of the class you will know the core components inside computers, their functions, and how to install them into a computer.

Build a computer from scratch in 4 weeks

Have you ever wondered what is inside of a computer? Computers have become one of the largest industries in the last century, and you can now bring yourself into that same industry. You will understand the basics for computer hardware, the history of computers, and how to build your own! Written by enterprise-level studio & electrical engineering veterans, our course will get you trained with the ability to construct your own computer farm, ready yourself for any job that uses desktop PCs, and building your daily driver for all things digital. (Also a great introduction for those interested in blockchain or cryptocurrency technology.)

If you are a local South LA business, Contact vsedc to learn how to become a client & apply for the program.

Computer Capabilities include:

  • Computer Enclosure
  • Motherboard
  • CPU
  • Memory / RAM
  • Hard Drive
  • Graphics Card or Integrated Graphics
  • Linux Operating System
